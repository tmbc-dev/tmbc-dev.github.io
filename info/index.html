Phase 1

Token Mined by Community (TMBC) is a project that lets users simulate the mining process on Binance Smart Chain.

To start mining:

  • Install a web3 wallet (e.g., Metamask, Trust Wallet).
  • Visit tmbc.cc/app to access your mining dashboard.
  • Upgrade your mining level by paying network+project fees or inviting new users with your referral link.

TMBC tokens are mined continuously, and rewards are automatically added to your balance every second—no manual claiming or fees required!
